Welcome to SEAR Group of Trusts

SEAR Group of Trusts is a non-profit organization committed to providing free medication to those who need it most. Our mission is to ensure that everyone has access to the medication they need to live healthy and productive lives.

Our Services

We provide free medication to individuals who are unable to afford it. Our services include:

  • Medication for chronic conditions, such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma
  • Medication for acute conditions, such as infections and injuries
  • Medication for mental health conditions, such as depression and anxiety

How We Help

Our team of healthcare professionals and volunteers work together to provide personalized care to each individual who seeks our help. We take the time to understand each patient's unique needs and circumstances, and work with them to find the best possible solution.

Get Involved

We rely on the generosity of donors and volunteers to help us continue our work. There are many ways to get involved, including:

  • Donating money to support our programs
  • Volunteering your time and skills to help us run our programs
  • Spreading the word about our organization and our mission

Contact Us

If you or someone you know is in need of free medication, please contact us to learn more about our services. You can also contact us to learn more about how you can get involved and support our mission.
